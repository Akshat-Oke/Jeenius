Pattern Allowance

Info here

Metals expand on heating and shrink when cooled. Cavity filled with molten metal will produce a casting smaller in size.

  1. Shrinkage Allowance or Contraction Allowance
  2. Machining Allowance or Finishing Allowance
  3. Draft Allowance or Taper Allowance
  4. Rapping Allowance or Shake Allowance – negative allowance
  5. Distortion Allowance or Camber Allowance
